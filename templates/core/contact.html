Frequently Asked Questions

To create a playlist, log in to your account and click on the "Playlists" tab in the navigation menu. Then click the "Create Playlist" button, enter a name and description for your playlist, and choose whether it should be public or private. You can then add songs to your playlist from our music library.

If you encounter any technical issues while using MusicWave, please use the contact form on this page to report the problem. Include as much detail as possible, including the device and browser you're using, and any error messages you received. Our technical team will investigate and get back to you as soon as possible.

Currently, MusicWave is a streaming-only platform, and downloading music for offline listening is not available. However, we are working on implementing this feature in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

To update your account information, log in to your account and click on your profile picture in the top right corner. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu. From there, you can update your name, bio, and profile picture. Don't forget to save your changes when you're done.
